Security features and policies

Customer trust and data security are critical to everything we do at SerpApi.

  • Encryption

    100% of our website pages, API requests, and resources go through HTTPS. Our SSL certificate is issued by Cloudflare, Inc (ECC CA-3). Encrypted and authenticated using QUIC, X25519, and AES_128_GCM.

  • Password and Credential Storage

    SerpApi made it impossible for anyone to access our user passwords. We use one-way password hashing using the BCrypt hash algorithm for every user. We produce audit logs for all critical parts of SerpApi.

  • Data Security

    AWS S3 and database security (multiple redundancy, encryption in transit even between local servers). We follow a Zero Trust Network doctrine: All of our VPS are assumed to be exposed via the Internet.

All our APIs are covered

All of our APIs are covered by the international specialist insurers Hiscox and RGS.

  • Professional Liability E&O Insurance

    We take extra precautions to guarantee our client’s business continuity in case of our objective fault. It's the ultimate shield against professional liability risks.

  • General Liability Insurance

    Our General Liability Insurance coverage provides a safety net, shielding our clients from the financial burdens of third-party bodily injury, property damage, or advertising claims.

  • Cybersecurity Insurance

    Our cybersecurity insurance covers data breaches, cyberattacks, and privacy threats with comprehensive incident response and liability coverage. - via RGS
